# Acceptable Use Policy Draft

This policy is a product draft and requires legal review before launch.

## Platform intent

Efi-G supports permissioned avatar and identity-media creation. Users may create with their own avatars, fictional characters, licensed creator avatars, and approved marketplace assets.

## Required conditions

Users must:

- comply with platform terms;
- use only assets they own or are licensed to use;
- respect creator license scopes;
- follow age and verification requirements;
- avoid deception, harassment, coercion, or exploitation;
- report suspected misuse.

## Forbidden use

The platform prohibits:

- illegal content or conduct;
- youth-related restricted content;
- non-permissioned real-person identity use;
- harassment, threats, stalking, or humiliation;
- impersonation for fraud or deception;
- hate or targeted abuse;
- attempts to bypass policy, verification, payment, or review controls;
- upload or training of assets without rights;
- redistribution outside license scope.

## Creator license controls

Creators can define allowed and blocked categories, audience, price, duration, review requirements, and revocation terms. Users may not exceed those terms.

## Enforcement

The platform may block jobs, remove assets, suspend accounts, freeze payouts, revoke marketplace access, preserve evidence, or report unlawful activity where required.

## Appeals

Users and creators may appeal enforcement decisions. Appeals require account identity, affected asset or job identifier, and a short explanation.
