# 01 — Brand Positioning

## Brand territory

Efi-G is a permissioned identity-media platform. It is not a raw generator, open social feed, or unrestricted model host. It is a controlled projection layer where likeness, character, creator licensing, and AI media generation are treated as governed assets.

## Brand concepts

### Primary concept: Efi-G

Possible meanings:

- Expressive Fantasy Identity Governance
- Embodied Fictional Interface — Governed
- Efi-G as a short consumer brand backed by a serious rights layer

### Category phrase

**Permissual Projection** means permissioned projection of self, character, or creator avatar.

## Tagline options

1. Your avatar. Your rules.
2. Private projection, permissioned sharing.
3. Create yourself without giving yourself away.
4. The governed avatar marketplace.

## Recommended initial tagline

**Your avatar. Your rules.**

## Voice

The brand should feel private, premium, creator-positive, technically serious, and protective without being paternalistic.

## Brand values

### Consent

Permission is a living contract with scope, time, audience, revocation, and auditability.

### Sovereignty

Users and creators control the identity assets derived from them. The platform acts as steward, processor, and marketplace operator.

### Privacy

Privacy is an engineering primitive: data minimization, vault separation, key isolation, limited retention, and visible controls.

### Creativity

The product exists because people want expressive spaces that mainstream platforms rarely support. Creative freedom is valid when it is lawful, permissioned, and governed.

### Market fairness

Creator economics must be legible. Rates, platform fees, promotion packages, refund rules, and license boundaries must be easy to understand.

## Brand architecture

- Efi-G Studio: private generation and avatar creation.
- Efi-G Market: licensed creator avatars, packs, styles, accessories, and spaces.
- Efi-G Rooms: controlled shared experiences.
- Efi-G Vault: consent, identity, rights, and audit records.
- Efi-G Lab: model training, red-team testing, and policy research.

## Positioning statement

For users and creators who want expressive AI avatar media without losing control of identity or rights, Efi-G is a consent-first projection platform that combines private generation, creator licensing, and audit-grade governance.
